Media and Film

Growing up I have always been infatuated with cameras and media equipmet. I wanted to persue a career in the film industry so I exposed myself to a tremendous amount of equipment, software, and practices in the media field. In highschool I founded the multimedia club and worked as a sports videographer for local access television. Later, in college, I managed and taught students about the equipment in the media lab. Now, I work as a film editor at B-Roll Films, a Boston based production company.

Seeds Of Stem

Seeds of Stem is a curriculum structure for children to learn valuable science, technicalogy and math concepts. I was hired to create a promotional and informative video showcasing this structure and highlighting it's importance. This docustyle piece gave me the opportunity to work filming interviews of subjects which involved shot framing, lighting and sound collection. I later edited such media using free to use software such as Davinci Resolve, Audacity and GIMP for graphics.

Alien - Scene Recreation

This shot for shot recreation of the classic sci fi thriller Alien, was by far the most fun I've had on a video project so far in my career. To pull this off, I had taken part in almost every role including set design, props, shot composition, actor casting, directing, and of course editing. I worked with a practical effects artist to create the Chest-burster and most of the gore in the scene. This was edited using Sony Vegas, After Effects, and Audacity.
